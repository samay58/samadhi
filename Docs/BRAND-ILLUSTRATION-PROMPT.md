# Samadhi in-app mark exploration prompt

This prompt is retained for a future transparent in-app emblem. The production Home Screen icon and its decision record live in `Docs/BRAND.md`.

Create an original, whimsical logo illustration for “Samadhi,” a refined native iPhone running app that lets music find the runner’s rhythm. Do not include any text, letters, app name, footwear, literal music notes, hearts, generic fitness symbols, or clip-art silhouettes.

Build one memorable emblem from a single continuous ribbon-like gesture. It should subtly suggest two offset footsteps, a musical pulse, and forward motion without resolving into any one of them. The form should feel alive, light, and slightly mischievous, with an elegant imbalance that makes it feel drawn by a human rather than generated from perfect geometry.

Art direction: editorial illustration meets precision iOS iconography. Use a warm parchment, clay orange, muted plum, apricot, and soft olive palette. Give the mark tactile depth through translucent enamel, a restrained internal glow, one sharp specular highlight, and delicate edge shading. Keep the silhouette crisp at small sizes. Avoid amorphous gradient blobs, inflated 3D mascots, chrome, neon, cyberpunk styling, glossy stock-icon rendering, excessive symmetry, and busy detail.

Composition: one centered emblem with generous transparent space around it. No background scene. No border. No rounded-square app-icon container. The result must work as a small navigation mark above a full-screen fluid interface and remain legible at 24 points.

Deliver a high-resolution square PNG with a transparent background. Also show a second monochrome silhouette version in dark warm ink on transparent background for accessibility and reduced-transparency contexts. Preserve the exact same silhouette across both versions.

# Samadhi brand system

## Meaning

Samadhi names meditative consciousness. The product should feel absorbed, steady, and present while the runner's movement and music settle into one rhythm.

The product name is Samadhi. The phrase “in step” remains a lowercase run metric, not a second brand or wordmark.

## Icon decision

The selected direction is the compact interlocking ribbon supplied in the first user concept. It won over the vertical alternative because its silhouette remains distinct at Home Screen size, its loops imply continuity without becoming a literal music note, and its horizontal balance fits the app's calm character.

The production master is `Resources/Assets.xcassets/AppIcon.appiconset/Samadhi-AppIcon-1024.png`.

- Opaque 1024 by 1024 PNG
- Warm parchment field with no container border
- Emblem occupies approximately 69 percent of the square after optical scaling
- Apricot, orange, plum, olive, and ivory palette
- Controlled satin depth rather than high-gloss plastic rendering
- No text, literal runner, music note, or fitness symbol

The 180-pixel and 60-pixel review renders are stored in `Evidence/Brand/`. The 180-pixel render is the meaningful Home Screen check for a 60-point icon on a 3x iPhone display.

## In-app mark

The small native ribbon glyph remains the in-app navigation mark for this milestone. It echoes the app icon without placing a miniature square icon inside the interface. A later transparent production emblem may replace it only after 24-point legibility and reduced-transparency checks pass.

## GitHub cover

The final cover artwork was generated in Midjourney and supplied by the user. It uses a wide interwoven rhythm form over a parchment topographic field. The exact lowercase phrase “music in stride” sits in the open upper-left field, outside the sculpture, in a soft old-style italic. Its forward posture evokes flow while the restrained plum tone keeps the ribbon dominant at social-card size.

- Original Midjourney artwork: `Brand/GitHub-Cover-Midjourney-Original.png`
- Typeset high-resolution master: `Brand/GitHub-Cover-Samadhi-Master.png`
- GitHub-ready 1280 by 640 export: `Brand/GitHub-Cover-Samadhi-1280x640.png`

The rejected inside-loop placement is preserved under `Brand/Iterations/` for decision history. It read as a label attached to the object and used an upright serif that felt too static.

Keep “music in stride” as the public tagline. It is shorter, more self-possessed, and closer to the product's feeling than the earlier line “Music meets your stride.”

## Generation record

The production icon master was refined with the built-in image-generation tool from the first user-provided concept. The prompt preserved the connected silhouette, reduced the toy-like gloss, moved the mark to a warm parchment field, and prohibited text, mockup framing, literal music symbols, and rounded-square artwork. The result was center-cropped from 1254 to 1080 pixels before final 1024-pixel export so the emblem reads confidently without entering Apple's mask-risk area.
